About Me

Additional Information

Dr. Eman Zeid holds DBA in Human Resources Management from Westbrook University, USA in collaboration with Ein Shams University and Executive MBA with honors from the Arab Academy for Sciences & Technology.  Dr. Zeid is a freelance professional HR & Management Consultant. She has started her career with diversified multinational organizations for 20 years as HR Director whereby she gained a wide-range management exposure and expertise in growth strategy Initiatives, HR Management, Turnaround Leadership and mega mergers and acquisitions. Currently, she is working in consultation and training for over 15 years with Banks (CIB and Banque Misr), Pepsi, AUC, TMG (Talaat Mostafa Group), government sector companies and more.   She has an accreditation certificate as a Change Agent from the Implementation Management Associates Inc., USA in "Accelerating Implementation Methodology". She is also a Master Trainer for the TTT Leadership Certificate from the School of Continuing Education, Wisconsin Milwaukee University, USA, holds the Certificate of Proficiency, in Human Resources – American Institute of Professional Studies, August 2019.
